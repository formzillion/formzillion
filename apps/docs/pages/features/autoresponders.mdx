# Autoresponders

The autoresponding feature in Formzillion allows you to automatically send a response to users who submit a form. This feature is useful for sending confirmation messages, thank-you notes, or any other kind of message that you want to automatically send to users.

## Steps to setup Autoresponders

To set up an autoresponder, follow these steps:

1. Log in to your Formzillion account.
2. Create a new form or select an existing one.
3. Navigate to the "**Settings**" > "**Autoresponders**".
4. Under the "**Autoresponders**" section, click on toggle button for "**Autoresponders**".
   <br />
   <center>![image](/screenshots/autoresponders1.png)</center>
   <br />
5. Click on toggle button for "**Use Template**".
   <br />
   <center>![image](/screenshots/autoresponders2.png)</center>
   <br />
6. Select pre defined templetes for Autoresponder.
   <br />
   <center>![image](/screenshots/autoresponders3.png)</center>
   <br />
7. Click on the "**Save**" button to save your autoresponder settings.

## Managing Autoresponders

You can disable your autoresponders by following these steps:

1. Log in to your Formzillion account.
2. Click on the form that you want to manage.
3. Click on the "**Settings**" button.
4. Navigate to the "**Settings**" > "**Autoresponders**".
5. Under the "**Autoresponders**" section, click on toggle button for "Autoresponders".
   <br />
   <center>![image](/screenshots/autoresponders1.png)</center>
   <br />

6.Click on the "**Save**" button to save your autoresponder settings.
